The Best Movies According to Ana Paula

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Notting Hill

It's a romantic comedy set in the picturesque neighborhood of Notting Hill in London. The plot revolves around William Thacker, played by Hugh Grant, an ordinary bookstore owner whose life takes a turn when Anna Scott, a famous Hollywood actress played by Julia Roberts, walks into his shop.

Home Alone

It's a classic family comedy that follows the story of Kevin McCallister, played by Macaulay Culkin, an eight-year-old boy who is accidentally left behind when his family departs for Christmas vacation.

The Notebook

is a poignant love story based on Nicholas Sparks' novel. The plot unfolds in two timelines: the story of Noah Calhoun, played by Ryan Gosling, and Allie Hamilton, played by Rachel McAdams, as they meet and fall in love in the 1940s, and their reunion in the 2000s when they are elderly.
